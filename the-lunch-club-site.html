How it works

Three simple steps.

One

We plan your menu

Our nutritionists design balanced weekly menus based on your child's age, preferences, and dietary needs — grounded in the latest evidence-based guidelines.

Two

We cook and pack it

Every meal is made fresh using whole, unprocessed ingredients and packed in scientifically vetted, plastic-free packaging that's safe for little hands.

Three

A full week of lunches at your door each Sunday. Grab, pack, and send them off — knowing exactly what's inside.

Our promise

What we stand for.

Zero processed ingredients

Whole foods only. If it has an ingredient list longer than five words, it doesn't belong in a lunchbox.

Evidence-based nutrition

Menus reviewed quarterly against the latest paediatric nutrition research — not trends, not guesswork.

Safe, clean packaging

No BPA, no microplastics. All packaging meets the most stringent current safety standards.

Kid-tested, parent-trusted

We won't serve a meal our own kids wouldn't eat. Nutrition and delicious are not opposites.
